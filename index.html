SMMO Scaler

by Alpha

Created by Alpha

A gear optimization calculator for SimpleMMO players. Analyzes available items and ranks them by power to help you make informed purchasing decisions.

Key Features

  • Calculates the best equipment based on your level and gold
  • Power formula considers Strength, Defence, and Crit% values
  • Configurable stat weights with presets
  • Local API logging - futureproof and hassle-free
  • Community-maintained item database

Open Source

This tool is free and open-source. The database can be maintained by anyone, ensuring it stays current with game updates. If you find it useful, a simple thank you makes my day!

How It Works
  1. Enter your level and how much gold you can spend per item.
  2. Adjust stat weights to match your playstyle, or pick a preset.
  3. Hit Find Best Gear to see the top 5 items per slot.
  4. Switch between Power (raw strength) and Value (best bang for your gold) sorting.
  5. Enable Budget Cap to find the best full loadout within your total gold.

Power formula: Each stat is multiplied by its weight slider. Crit uses Level x 2 x (Crit / 1000) x Weight.

Unavailable items may be stronger but have no market listing. Tools and Interesting Items are shown in their own sections.

All data is estimated from the last database update.
